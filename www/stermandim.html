Rules

The Rules are simple: You need to guess a combination of 4 colors. You have the choice between 6 colors. You have to guess before 8 rounds otherwise it's lose. Dots on the right side of the board will give some indication on how many colors you have well guess (red dot) or how many colors are good but not in the right place(white dot). The order of the dots does not correspond of the order of the combination.
